About this project...

Hi everyone! Welcome to my hobby Web-page!


I've decided to build this Web-site in order to demostrate the technologies I work with to potential collaborators, colleagues and employers..

About me...

My name is Maxim. Nice to meet you!


Breifly about myself: I am ENT Doctor and Scientist. In addition to Medical Doctors' degree and PhD in Medical Science I possess Engineers' Degree in Information&Measurement Technologies. I'm author of 17 Russian Federation Patents and more than 100 scientific papers.

I've been also occupied with programming as hobby since early childhood. I love web-programming (I used to build my own CMS with PHP, developed some small games in WebGL etc.), mobile development and computer graphics.

Nowadays, I'm focused mainly on Javascript, Unity3D and iOS development. However, as University Professor I supervise students' research on various subjects from audio/visual analysis in C++ (OpenCV) to hardware programming (Arduino, Raspberry etc.). As part of my daily routine I've been using Engineering software such as LabView, MathCad, MathLab etc.

I really love visualisations and computer graphics and utilize them a lot for my scientific projects and research papers. I used to create with Xara and 3DSMax, but since recently I moved to Blender 2.8. It is really powerfull and fully compatible with Unity3D!

I would really appreciate any collaboration and joint activities!
