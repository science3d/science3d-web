Schematics of Blood-brain barrier & Drug Delivery systems I prepared for our acrtice in Advanced Heathcare Materials journal (2018)

I am an author of more than 100 research papers. Below you can see links for some of my latest peer-reviewed articles:

cGMP-dependent protein kinase I in vascular smooth muscle cells improves ischemic stroke outcome in mice / Journal of Cerebral Blood Flow and Metabolism 2019 (Q1, IF = 6.04) Delivery and Use of Nano-and Microcarriers for Cell Functionalization / Advanced Healthcare Materials 2018, Vol. 7, Issue 3, Article number 1700818. (Q1, IF = 6.27)

My idea was to create automatic system that will monitor and track mice underwent MCAO surgery (to model stroke) to collect information on stoke outcomes. I delegated this task to students as their Master's thesis research at Tomsk Polytechnic University. The system consisted of hardware that we installed on the cage and tracking software based on openCV. The system proved to be effective in evaluation general activity in mice as well as motor deficits after stroke. The system is free to use and technical documentation is available upon request.

About me...
